About Slit-Scan Editor

Chrome Extension

What is Slit-Scan Editor?

Slit-Scan Editor is a Chrome extension that brings the classic slit-scan photography technique to your browser. Create mesmerizing time-based visual effects from your images with just a few clicks.

Is my image uploaded to a server?

No. All processing happens locally in your browser using Canvas 2D API. Your image never leaves your device.

What's the maximum image size?

There are no artificial limits, but very large images may affect performance depending on your device's memory.

Can I use the results commercially?

Yes, you retain full rights to all images you create using Slit-Scan Editor.
