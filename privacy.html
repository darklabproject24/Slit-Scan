Privacy Policy for Slit-Scan Effect Editor

Last Updated: December 28, 2025

Overview

Slit-Scan Effect Editor is a Chrome extension that allows users to apply slit-scan motion blur effects to their images. This privacy policy explains our approach to user privacy and data handling.

Data Collection

We collect ZERO data.

This extension does not collect, store, transmit, or share any user information or data of any kind.

Specifically:

  • We do NOT collect personal information
  • We do NOT collect browsing history
  • We do NOT collect usage statistics or analytics
  • We do NOT use cookies or tracking technologies
  • We do NOT transmit any data to external servers

Image Processing

All image processing occurs entirely on your local device:

  • Images you upload are processed in your browser's memory only
  • Images are NEVER uploaded to any external server
  • Images are NEVER stored by the extension
  • When you close the editor, all image data is immediately discarded

Permissions

This extension requires no special permissions:

  • It cannot access your browsing data
  • It cannot access the websites you visit
  • It cannot access files on your computer (except images you explicitly upload)
  • It cannot communicate with external servers

Third-Party Services

This extension does NOT use any third-party services, analytics, or tracking tools.

Data Storage

This extension does NOT store any data:

  • No localStorage usage
  • No sessionStorage usage
  • No IndexedDB usage
  • No cookies
  • Exported images are saved directly to your chosen download location

Children's Privacy

This extension does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change this privacy policy, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this document. However, our commitment to zero data collection will not change.
